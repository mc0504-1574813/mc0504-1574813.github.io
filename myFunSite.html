Background

Our company was founded by two Seattle natives who love trying out and making new recipes. Their passion for food lead to the birth of this company in which the mission is connecting food lovers from around the world and sharing fun recipes. If you are interested in making delicious dishes for your family or just enjoy food, please join this community today and you will find yourself truly surrounding by people just like you.

Summerhill Crab Salad

Check out this new crab salad recipe from Jennifer Brown. It blends together newly-cut greens, fresh crab stripes and other nutritious vegetables such as carrots and parsley. With a few drops of lemon juice on top, this refreshing dish is a perfect fit for summer.

It's summer! What does that mean: a fruit tart! A perfect peach and raspberry tart with mango coulis, credit to the amazing chef Mother Jessie. Fresh fruits, rich cream cheese filling all above a plain sweet crust; what more could one ask for?
